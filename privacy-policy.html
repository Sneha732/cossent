Privacy Policy – Cosscent

Welcome to Cosscent, India’s luxury cosmetics and perfume brand. At Cosscent, we value your trust and are committed to protecting your privacy and safeguarding your personal data.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect the personal information of our customers, partners, and website visitors. It also details your rights under applicable privacy laws, including the Digital Personal Data Protection (DPDP) Act, 2023 and international standards like GDPR.

1. Objective, Scope & Applicability

This Privacy Policy applies to:

  • Customers purchasing products through our website, retail stores, or authorized sellers.
  • Users interacting with our brand online, via social media, or at offline events.
  • Partners, suppliers, and third-party manufacturers who assist in our operations.

2. Definitions

  • Personal Data: Any information that identifies an individual.
  • Cookies: Small files that enhance your browsing experience.
  • Third Parties: External entities like logistics providers, payment processors, or marketing agencies.

3. Information We Collect

  • Personal Identification Data – Name, email, phone, address.
  • Transaction Data – Order history, invoices, payment details.
  • Technical Data – IP address, browser, cookies.
  • Preferences – Product reviews, survey responses.

4. How We Collect Data

We collect information directly from you, through cookies, third-party services, and offline events.

5. Purpose of Data Collection

  • Order fulfillment and delivery
  • Personalized recommendations
  • Exclusive offers and promotions
  • Fraud detection and legal compliance

6. Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell your data. We share only with logistics partners, payment gateways, marketing agencies, or legal authorities when required.

7. Security Measures

We use SSL encryption, firewalls, and regular audits to safeguard your data.

8. Your Rights

  • Access, correction, and deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent for marketing
  • File grievances
