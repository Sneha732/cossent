How can I track my order?

Once your order is shipped, you will receive a tracking number via email. You can use this tracking number to track the progress of your shipment on our website.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit and debit cards, UPI, net banking, PayPal, and select wallet payments to make checkout smooth and secure.

What is your return policy?

If you’re not completely satisfied with your purchase, you can return unused products within 7–14 days of delivery (depending on our return guidelines). Please ensure items are in their original condition and packaging. Refunds are processed after inspection.

Are your products cruelty-free?

Yes! All of our products are 100% cruelty-free and never tested on animals. We are committed to ethical and sustainable practices.
